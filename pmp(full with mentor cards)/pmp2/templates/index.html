FAQ

Why should I become a mentor with PMP?

This would be your first opportunity as an alumnus/alumna to help the student community and assist the future batches with your knowledge and experience. Becoming a mentor with PMP will allow you to directly contribute to the success of your juniors and at the same time make you stay connected to the institute.

What is the further procedure after I register?

After the mentor and mentee registrations, mentees will be allocated to you in your preferred career field. You will have the liberty to choose the number of mentees that you wish to guide. Your respective mentees will then initiate the conversation with you and you can mutually decide on the way ahead. We will have a few follow-ups to assist you through any difficulties.

How will my personal details be used?

Details such as name, roll number and contact details will remain confidential till the allocation procedure. Only the allocated mentees will be given your contact details. During mentee registration, only your work profile will be made public.

What is new this time?

  • Mentees will have an option to fill in their preferences for the mentor (based on the work profile).
  • The allocation will also allow cross-department mentor-mentee pairing
  • A system to refer your batchmates who you feel could contribute to this program by becoming mentors.

How did this program benefit the mentee last year?

Based on the feedback received, the mentees were highly benefited from the program last year. The guidance they got from their mentors helped them throughout the placement process.
